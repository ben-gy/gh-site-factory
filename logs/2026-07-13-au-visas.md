## Architecture Decisions
- **Pivot-cache extraction (the crux):** the source `.xlsx` is an Excel *pivot table*; the full 303,615-row fact table lives inside the embedded pivot **cache** (`pivotCacheDefinition1.xml` + `pivotCacheRecords1.xml`, 24 MB). The pipeline unzips the workbook with `fflate` and parses the cache XML by hand (regex over `<r>` records, shared-item indexing). Two subtle parser bugs were found & fixed during dev: (1) `split('<cacheField')` also split the `<cacheFields>` container, injecting a phantom field and shifting every cell by one; (2) a strict `v="..."` item regex dropped shared items carrying extra attributes. After fixing, totals matched the official figures exactly (2,880,320 at 2026-05-31).
- **Aggregation:** rolled the fact table into compact JSON — per-country series + latest category breakdown (117 KB), per-category series (13 KB), per-subclass series (23 KB), and a meta file. All browser-friendly; no runtime API calls.
- Vanilla TS over React — single-page tabbed tool, hand-rolled SVG for treemap/trends/matrix/Sankey; Leaflet only for the map. Copied canonical patterns (`squarify`, `tooltip`, `svgZoom`, `leafletMap`) rather than re-rolling.
- 7 views chosen for the data's shape (origin × category × time): Insights, world choropleth, nationalities leaderboard, category→subclass treemap, stacked-area trends, nationality×visa matrix, nationality→visa Sankey, plus a hash-linkable drill-down.
