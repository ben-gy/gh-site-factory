## Site Details
- **Name:** NEM Watch
- **Repo:** ben-gy/nemwatch
- **Category:** energy
- **Audience:** Australian households with solar/batteries, energy analysts, renewable energy advocates
- **Stack:** Vanilla TypeScript + Vite 6 + Vitest
- **Data strategy:** runtime-fetch (AEMO public JSON API) + localStorage for 24h history

## Data Sources
- AEMO NEM Summary API: `https://visualisations.aemo.com.au/aemo/apps/api/report/ELEC_NEM_SUMMARY`
  - Provides per-region spot price ($/MWh), total demand (MW), scheduled + semi-scheduled generation (MW), interconnector flows
  - 5 regions: NSW1, QLD1, SA1, TAS1, VIC1
  - Updates every 5 minutes (settlement intervals)
  - No auth required

## Architecture Decisions
- Vanilla TypeScript over React: single-page monitoring dashboard, no complex component tree or routing needed
- No external chart library: custom SVG rendering in `chart.ts` keeps the bundle tiny (14KB JS, 5KB gzip)
- localStorage for history: no backend needed; chart populates as user keeps the tab open (max 288 points = 24h)
- Dark theme: target audience is technical/monitoring (analysts, battery-optimising households checking at night)
- Semi-scheduled generation used as a renewable proxy: this is the AEMO category for variable wind/solar
