# App Ideas

Add app ideas here. The scheduled task will pick the first idea from this list, build it, and remove it. One idea per line. Be as detailed or brief as you like - the task will flesh it out.

- Machinery of Government (AU) — interactive explorer of how the Australian federal government AND each state/territory government is structured: portfolios, departments, agencies, statutory bodies, and ministers, plus how Machinery of Government (MoG) changes over time have renamed/merged/split departments (e.g. via Administrative Arrangements Orders). Use official sources like directory.gov.au (federal Government Online Directory has a data feed), data.gov.au, and state government directories.
- Intergovernmental Relations (AU) — machinery of government from Australia's perspective but outward-facing: how Australia formally relates to other governments. Treaties (Australian Treaties Database / austlii treaty library), diplomatic posts and DFAT embassy network, free trade agreements, international organisation memberships, sister-agency arrangements, and bilateral relationship views per country. Network graph of Australia's government-to-government relationships.
